Meet our Staff

Beth Britton:Beth Britton graduated Fones School of Dental Hygiene at the Universtiy of Bridgeport. Practicing clinical hygiene, she gained in-depth knowledge of practice styles and dynamics. Realizing the demand for a company dedicated to aiding practices with their staffing needs, Beth formed Dental Fillings, Inc. She has been serving the dental community since 1987 as an employment agent and consultant. Beth takes pride in developing long-term relationships with her clients, this affords her the opportunity to provide exceptional insights and help build successful practices.

Barbra Ann Keogh: Barbara has been associated with Dental Fillings for over 20 years. Throughout her career as a Registered Dental Assistant. she has worked continuously through the years for various specialty offices, as well as general practices. Also, she has for the past 25 years been an instructor at Brookdale Community College in the evenings, educating students into the Dental profession. Now with Dental Fillings full time her extensive knowledge plays an important role going forward in this profession.

Michele Patruno-Volpini:In 1998 while attending UMDNJ's dental hygene program, Michele began her career with Dental Fillings. Over the years while she completed her bachelors and masters degree, at Monclair State University, Michele continued to provide temporary dental hygene services.Through her 15 years of experience as a professional temp Michele has gained vital insight into dental practices throughout New Jersey. Her experience, knowledge and education give her the abiliy to assist clients with their unique staffing needs..

Sabrina Klitsch:Sabrina joined Dental Fillings in 2004. Her previous dental career involved training as a dental assistant and dental receptionist in a private practice. Though involved in many aspects of Dental Fillings, Sabrina has the important task of payroll processing. When not working, Sabrina enjoys spending time with her children and grandchildren.
