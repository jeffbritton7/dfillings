Welcome to Dental Fillings Inc.

We are a professional dental placement service hat has been helping dental auxiliaries find employment and dental offices fill positions since 1985. More than two thousand dental practices have trusted us in their search.

Whether it's the convenience of our 'round-the-clock' telephone coverage or the appeal of being able to focus on dentistry as we interview and screen candidates, New Jersey's dental industry has relied on Dental Fillings for over two decades.
