For the Dental Auxiliary

Dental Fillings is New Jersey's premier dental placement service specializing in the permanent and temporary placement of dental hygienists, assistants, receptionists, office managers, and dentists.

Whether you are looking for your first job, are new to the area, or simply wish to work a few of your open days, Dental Fillings has a comprehensive list of employment opportunities to suit your needs.

Our experience with more than 2,000 dental practices over the past two decades ensures that we can find a position to match your skills, schedule, and location requirements.

Dental Fillings, Inc. is owned and operated by dedicated dental professionals with many years of dental office experience. We understand your needs and concerns and are motivated to find the best opportunity for you in the most efficient and expeditious manner.

Dedicated dental professionals working full-time to meet your needs Round the clock telephone coverage Weekly payroll system Insurance coverage for per diem staff includes Malpractice, Liability, and Workman's Compensation Servicing the dental community since 1985



Permanent Positions

Let Dental Fillings eliminate the hassles and uncertainties of the employment process. All it takes is one short interview at our office, and we will locate the position that best suits your needs. We will help you select the best opportunities and arrange interviews at convenient times..

All this is done without a fee!

Temporary positions

Temporary assignments offer you the flexibility to work at your convenience, at the locations and hours you choose.
